Who Drives?

Free rides to covered medical appointments for Kentucky Medicaid members.

Last verified against KYTC: Aug. 22, 2025

How It Works

  1. Select your county below.
  2. Call the toll‑free broker number to schedule.
  3. Have your Medicaid ID, pickup/clinic addresses, date/time and return time.

Scheduling window (per KYTC): Mon–Fri 8:00–4:30, Sat 8:00–1:00. Service typically runs Mon–Fri 6:00–8:00, Sat 8:00–1:00. Policies can change—confirm when you call.

Find your transportation broker

Pick from the dropdown or type your county name, then press Enter or choose a match.

What To Say

“Hi, I have Kentucky Medicaid and need a ride to a doctor’s appointment on [date/time] from [pickup] to [clinic]. My county is [county]. I also need a return ride.”

  • Mention wheelchair or escort needs when you book.
  • Call back ASAP if details change.

If You Have Problems

HSTD Customer Service Hotline: 1‑888‑941‑7433 (Kentucky Transportation Cabinet).
