Who Accepts?

Once you’re approved, here’s how to know where you can use your Medicaid card.

  • Call your provider

    The easiest way is to ask directly. Call your doctor, clinic, hospital or pharmacy and ask if they take Kentucky Medicaid.

  • Search the official directory

    Use the state’s provider search to find doctors, clinics and specialists who accept Medicaid.

    Search providers on kynect.ky.gov

  • Pharmacies & Urgent Care

    Many large pharmacy chains and urgent care centers accept Medicaid, but it’s best to call ahead before your visit.
