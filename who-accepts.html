Who Accepts?

Finding providers that take Medicaid

Not every doctor, dentist, or hospital accepts Kentucky Medicaid. Here’s how to check before your visit.

1) Use your MCO directory

If you’re enrolled in a Managed Care Organization (MCO), each plan has its own online provider directory. Search for doctors, dentists, specialists, or hospitals near you.

2) Call ahead

Always call the provider’s office to confirm they accept Kentucky Medicaid (and your MCO, if applicable). Policies can change.

3) Pharmacies

Most major pharmacy chains and many local pharmacies accept Kentucky Medicaid for prescriptions. Bring your Medicaid card each time.

4) Dental & vision

Coverage varies by age and program. Confirm with providers whether routine dental or vision services are included under your plan.

5) Hospitals

Most hospitals in Kentucky accept Medicaid, but confirm coverage for specialty care or elective procedures before scheduling.

Tip: Start with your MCO’s member services number (on the back of your Medicaid card) for help finding in-network providers.
