Frequently Asked Questions

Who qualifies for Medicaid in Kentucky?

Eligibility depends on income, household size, age, disability, and other factors. Check the current rules on the state’s benefits website.

See eligibility and apply at kynect.ky.gov

What documents count as proof?

Residency: recent mail, lease, or a utility bill with your KY address.
Income: recent pay stubs or benefits letters.
Identity/SSN: Social Security number for each person applying.

How long does an application take?

It varies. Online is usually fastest if your documents are ready. Phone or in‑person help is available if you need it.

How do I renew or report changes?

Most people renew every year. If you move, change jobs, or your household changes, report it right away so your benefits stay accurate.

Manage your case at kynect.ky.gov or call 1‑855‑306‑8959.

What if I lost my documents?

You can still start your application. Ask staff about alternate proofs or how to get replacements.

Find a local DCBS office

Is this an official state website?

No. This is a plain‑language guide made to help Kentuckians understand the basics. For official information and applications, use the links above.
