MCO Plans

Kentucky Medicaid managed care plans

Most Kentucky Medicaid members are enrolled in a Managed Care Organization (MCO). Your plan handles your benefits, provider network, and prior authorizations.

Aetna Better Health of Kentucky

Offers medical, behavioral health, dental, and vision benefits. Member services number is on the back of your Medicaid ID card.

Anthem Blue Cross and Blue Shield

Kentucky Medicaid plans include medical, pharmacy, and behavioral health coverage. Member services number is on the back of your card.

CareSource Kentucky

Nonprofit managed care organization focused on Medicaid and low-income populations. Member services number is on your card.

Molina Healthcare of Kentucky

Medicaid specialist with a Kentucky provider network covering medical, dental, and pharmacy. Member services number is on your card.

Website

WellCare of Kentucky

Kentucky Medicaid plans include medical, behavioral health, and transportation benefits. Member services number is on your card.

Website

How to check or change your plan

  • Log in to kynect to see your current plan and coverage dates
  • Official Kentucky MCO list (CHFS)
  • Find free local help from a kynector

Tip: Your member services number and provider directory are on the back of your Medicaid ID card and on your plan’s website.
