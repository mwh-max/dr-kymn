How to Apply

Choose the route that’s easiest for you. Bring the “Must‑Haves” from the home page.

  • Online (fastest)

    Use the state’s benefits website. Create an account, fill out the form, and upload your documents.

    Apply online at kynect.ky.gov

  • By Phone

    Apply with a staff member on the phone. Keep your documents nearby.

    Call: 1‑855‑306‑8959

  • In Person

    Visit a local office. Ask about help scanning or copying your paperwork if needed.

    Find a local DCBS office
