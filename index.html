Hello, I'm Dr. KYMN

... the Kentucky Medicaid Notebook*. Medicaid is complicated. Lots of weird terms and rules. Here are some basics to get started.

Must‑Haves

These are required in most cases.

  • Medicaid ID Card

    If you have one, bring it with you.

  • Social Security Number

    For you and anyone in your household applying.

  • Proof of Kentucky Residency

    Recent mail, lease or utility bill with your KY address.

  • Proof of Income

    Recent pay stubs or benefits letters for your household.

  • Recent Medical Bills

    Optional but helpful for determining coverage needs.
