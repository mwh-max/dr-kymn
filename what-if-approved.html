What If I'm Approved?

Approval is the start of coverage. Here’s what to expect and how to use it.

What happens next

After approval, you’ll usually receive:

  • Approval notice in the mail
  • Medicaid ID card mailed separately
  • Managed Care Organization (MCO) enrollment packet

Your next steps

Make the most of your new coverage:

  • 1) Wait for your card

    Bring your Medicaid ID card to doctor visits and pharmacies. If it doesn’t arrive in a few weeks, call the number in your approval notice.

  • 2) Pick a provider

    Choose a primary care provider (PCP) in your MCO’s network. Use our Who Accepts? page to confirm.

  • 3) Learn your benefits

    Medicaid covers a wide range of services. Read your MCO’s handbook for details on doctor visits, prescriptions, dental, vision, and more.

  • 4) Report any changes

    Keep your address, phone, income, and household info up to date in kynect to avoid coverage issues.

  • 5) Watch for renewal

    Coverage isn’t permanent — you’ll need to renew each year. See our Renewal page for what to expect.
