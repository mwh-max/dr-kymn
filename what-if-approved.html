What If I'm Approved?

Great! Here’s what you do next

You’re approved for Kentucky Medicaid (or KCHIP). Take these quick steps to start using benefits smoothly.

1) Watch for your card

Your Medicaid ID card may arrive after the approval notice. Keep both in a safe place.

2) Create/confirm your account

Log in to kynect to review messages, coverage start dates, and managed care plan info.

3) Pick or confirm your plan

If you’re assigned a Managed Care Organization (MCO), confirm it or choose a different one during the allowed window.

4) Find providers

Use your plan’s directory to choose a primary care provider and dentist. Call ahead to confirm they accept Kentucky Medicaid.

5) Bring your card to visits

Show your Medicaid ID card at appointments and pharmacies. Ask about any copays or prior authorization needs.

6) Keep info current

Report changes to address, phone, income, household right away in kynect to avoid coverage interruptions.
