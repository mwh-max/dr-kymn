Renewal & Redetermination

Medicaid is reviewed every year. Here’s how to keep your coverage active.

Your yearly checklist

Plain‑language steps to get through renewal smoothly.

  • 1) Watch for your notice

    The state will mail a renewal packet or notice. Open it right away and note any deadline.

  • 2) Update your info first

    In kynect, confirm your address, phone, income, and household. Out‑of‑date info can cause a loss of coverage.

  • 3) Complete and return on time

    If the packet asks for documents (ID, income, etc.), upload or send them before the deadline.

  • 4) Watch your messages

    Check kynect for messages. If the state asks for more, respond quickly to avoid a gap in coverage.

  • 5) If you miss it

    If coverage closes because you missed a step, act fast. You may have a short window to submit what’s missing and restore coverage.

  • 6) Report changes year‑round

    Don’t wait for renewal to report changes. Update address, phone, income, and household whenever they change.

How do I upload documents or check my renewal?

Log in to kynect to see notices, upload files, and confirm your status. Keep copies of what you send and note the date.
